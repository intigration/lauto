ExxonMobil

Sustaining Capital Alliance, Baytown Complex

Providing alliance engineering services since 1996
Location: Baytown, Texas, United States
Operator: ExxonMobil
Duration: 1996 - ongoing
Scope: Front end development, project management, engineering, procurement and construction support

The alliance agreement reflects a long-term relationship with ExxonMobil which has developed and strengthened throughout the multiple decades that our companies have worked together. Our scope has grown to include the Baytown refinery, chemical plant and olefins complex.

Our services include front end loading, project management, engineering, procurement and construction support.

Our project sizes range between USD $20,000 and USD $70,000,000, with the majority of projects having a total installed cost of more than USD $2,000,000.
