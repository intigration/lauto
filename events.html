Where We'll Be Next

3–4 June | Aberdeen, Scotland

EIC Energy Exports Conference

Lean Automation is proud to participate in the EIC Energy Exports Conference (EEC) for another year, showcasing how our expertise is helping businesses navigate the evolving energy landscape. With a strong track record in delivering complex energy projects worldwide, we’re committed to supporting clients in expanding their global reach and delivering lower-carbon solutions.

At this year’s conference, we’ll highlight our capabilities in carbon capture, hydrogen, and decarbonisation technologies, demonstrating how we help clients optimise projects, enhance efficiency, and accelerate the path to net-zero. Join us as we explore new opportunities and collaborate on the future of energy exports.
