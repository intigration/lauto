CommissionAbility™

CommissionAbility™ is Lean Automation’s integrated digital platform, powered by Ennova AI, designed to streamline commissioning and start-up processes. It combines internal innovations with top industry software to deliver projects earlier, provide critical path visibility, and data-driven recommendations for every phase.

Consistent. Efficient. Predictable. Reliable

Why CommissionAbility™

At Lean Automation, commissioning is powered by experts. Utilising CommissionAbility™ alongside their decades of expertise and experience, our Centre of Excellence (CoE), management teams and technicians bring data-driven solutions to projects to achieve the best possible outcomes.

It provides:

Unique ecosystem of in-house and partnered tools
Comprehensive support for all project phases
Scalable solutions for complex projects
Templated procedures and real-time dashboards for project insight, giving you a real-time view of progress at any given moment

OUR PROCESSES

Data Management and Validation

Validating data early eliminates rework, reduces schedule risk, and improves quality. Verified drawings ensure site execution confidence, saving time and avoiding delays.

Benchmarking

Benchmarking key metrics—costs, performance, hours, and completions—helps optimise future projects by avoiding past errors and incorporating best practices.

Planning and Sequencing

Our commissioning readiness plan (CRP) aligns commissioning milestones with construction priorities, ensuring readiness and successful project transitions.

OUR DIGITAL TOOLS

Our tools are proven digital enablers. They significantly reduce planning, field and project management hours by providing live data, analysis, and prioritised workfronts.


CSU Logic Sequence Tool

  • Enables easy sequencing of tasks to align construction priorities with commissioning needs.

P&ID Extraction Tool

  • Automates data extraction, speeding up the transfer of P&ID data to the Systems Completion Database.

Commissioning Readiness Audit Tool

  • Audits projects using 2,000+ benchmarked criteria to identify risks and improve project success.
