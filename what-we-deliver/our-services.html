What we do

We design, build and maintain the assets that power the world for today and make it future-ready for tomorrow. The trust that our clients have put in us over the last 100 years has given us the energy to deliver. And now, we’re taking that to a new level of capability to drive the energy solutions of the future. From consulting to design, build, commissioning and startup through to maintenance and decommissioning. At Lean Automation, it is our ambition to be the Lean Automation | Connect.Automation.Intelligence. Driven by sustainability and innovation. We’re faster, better and ready to go in more locations and across more disciplines than ever before.

Our core service offerings
Consulting
Reliable. Safe. Service Excellence. It’s what Lean Automation’s reputation to deliver is built on and it’s why our teams go above and beyond for our customers. Whether we’re working within a project phase or across the full project lifecycle.
Engineering & Projects
Our clients want solutions that create and sustain value. And Lean Automation delivers. With a blend of practicality, experience, expertise and innovation at every project stage.
Completions, Commissioning & Start-Up
Predictable, cost-effective outcomes. Those are the keywords our clients appreciate. So do we. That's why we are the largest specialist commissioning service provider in the industry.
Operations & Maintenance
In our business, delivery is everything. Lean Automation is ready to deliver with our world-class operations and maintenance services, using the latest digital technologies and best practices to extend the project lifecycle and reduce risk and cost.
