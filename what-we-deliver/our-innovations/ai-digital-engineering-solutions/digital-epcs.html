Digital EPC’s

Lean Automation’s Digital EPC services are designed to transform how projects are designed, executed, and delivered by leveraging advanced AI & digital tools and methodologies. We bring a new level of precision, efficiency, and collaboration to Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC), ensuring that your assets are ready for the future.

Here’s how we support your digital transformation:

Digital FEED (Front-End Engineering Design)

We design and implement digital solutions at the earliest stages of your project, ensuring that your front-end engineering design is optimised for both efficiency and long-term value.

EPC 4.0 & Visualisation

Using our EPC4.0 integrated model, we bring a new level of real-time visualisation to project management. This allows for better decision-making, collaboration, and tracking of progress from anywhere in the world.

Asset Information Management

We manage and structure your asset’s information throughout the project lifecycle, ensuring that critical data is easily accessible and accurately maintained. This supports seamless operations and maintenance once the project is complete.

4D/5D Material Management

By integrating time (4D) and cost (5D) dimensions into material management, we provide a detailed, dynamic view of how materials are being used throughout the project, enabling better planning and reducing delays and cost overruns.

4D/5D Construction Sequencing

We apply 4D and 5D methodologies to your construction plans, allowing you to visualise how your project will unfold over time. This helps identify potential issues early and ensures that your project is delivered on schedule and within budget.

7D Project Digital Twin

Our 7D Digital Twin solution for projects brings together time, cost, and design data into a single model, providing a living, integrated and breathing digital replica of your project. This helps you monitor progress, optimise performance, and make data-driven decisions throughout the asset's lifecycle.

Immersive Design Review

We use our immersive technologies like virtual reality (VR) to review and refine project designs in real-time, allowing teams to collaborate more effectively and ensure that the design meets all requirements before construction begins.

Digital Telecom System Integrator

We provide fully integrated telecom systems to ensure that all communication networks for your project are digitally optimised, improving safety, efficiency, and connectivity.

Laser Scanning & Robotics

Utilising laser scanning and robotic technologies, we create accurate digital models of your assets, ensuring precision and reducing the risk of human error during project execution and handover.

Why Digital EPC's?

By embedding these advanced digital capabilities into every stage of the EPC process, we help you drive greater efficiencies, reduce costs, and achieve operational excellence through cutting-edge technology and industry-leading expertise.
