Digital Operations & Maintenance

Lean Automation’s Digital O&M services are powered by our own industrial Kentaverse, a suite of AI-driven solutions designed to unlock the full potential of your assets across their entire lifecycle. By leveraging cutting-edge generative AI and seamless integration with the best third-party systems, we bring the future of operations and maintenance to life.

Here’s how our custom-built solutions deliver smarter, more autonomous operations for your assets:

Advanced Process Control

Our AI-powered Advanced Process Control solution enables dynamic, real-time adjustments to your operations, optimising performance, reducing energy consumption, and ensuring that your processes run as efficiently as possible.

Industrial AI CoPilot

Lean Automation’s Industrial AI CoPilot is an intelligent assistant designed to support operators with real-time insights and decision-making support.

Immersive Training (AR/VR)

Using augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR), we offer immersive training solutions that enable your workforce to learn in safe, simulated environments.

Remote Operations Solutions

We enable your teams to operate critical systems and assets from any location, ensuring safe, reliable, and efficient remote operations, minimising the need for on-site personnel.

AI Geofencing for Safety

Our AI Geofencing technology enhances on-site safety by creating digital boundaries, alerting personnel when they enter restricted or hazardous areas, and ensuring compliance with safety protocols

Generative AI Solutions

We harness the power of generative AI to create custom solutions tailored to your specific operational challenges.

8D Digital Twin

Our 8D Digital Twin solution goes beyond traditional models by integrating real-time data with predictive analytics, simulating future performance and helping you make proactive, data-driven decisions.

Aging to Digital Assets

We transform aging, legacy systems and assets into digitally connected assets, ensuring that they are equipped to perform at optimal levels in the digital age. Our solutions modernise your operations without the need for costly overhauls.

Asset Performance Management

We leverage AI to monitor asset health, predict maintenance needs, and optimise performance..
