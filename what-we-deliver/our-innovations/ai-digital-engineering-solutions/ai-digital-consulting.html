AI & Digital Consulting

Lean Automation’s AI & Digital Consulting services are designed to guide your assets into the digital future with strategic precision. We help you harness the power of digital transformation to improve your operations, increase efficiency, and drive sustainable growth.

Here’s how we do it:

AI & Digital Advisory

Our experts provide strategic advice on Return on Investment (RoI) evaluations, market studies, and managing the change that comes with adopting new digital technologies.

Digital Use Cases FDS

We help define the functional design specifications (FDS) for digital use cases, ensuring that each solution is tailored to your specific operational needs.

Digital Project Management Consultants (PMC)

Our specialist project management consultants ensure that your digital initiatives are executed smoothly, on time, and within budget. We guide your teams through the complexities of digital transformation.

Industrial Cybersecurity Studies

In a world where digital assets are becoming more integrated, safeguarding them is critical. Our industrial cybersecurity experts assess and mitigate risks, ensuring that your digital infrastructure is secure from cyber threats.

Assets Digital Maturity Assessment

We assess where you are on your digital journey, analysing your asset’s digital maturity and identifying gaps to align your strategy with future goals.

Digital Twin Simulations for Feasibility Studies

We use digital twin simulations to assess the feasibility of your next project. These dynamic models allow you to predict how changes will affect your assets.

Why AI & Digital Consulting?

With our AI & Digital Consulting services, you’re not just adopting technology – you’re embedding it into the fabric of your business. From strategic planning and technology integration to process improvements and change management, we ensure that your digital transformation is smooth, secure, and successful. Whether you're at the start of your digital journey or looking to enhance your existing systems, we bring the expertise needed to future-proof your assets.
