Our Innovations

Centre of Excellence

Lean Automation Global Centre of Excellence

Technology-driven solutions are becoming the norm in our industry. More than ever, customers expect us to assess and adopt innovation to optimise our performance and create more value. The Lean Automation Global Centre of Excellence (CoE) helps keep us at the forefront of change in every strand of our business.

Our CoE acts as a hub at Lean Automation for the exploration and adoption of new technologies, tools, techniques, and practices. It sets us apart, giving us opportunity to elevate governance, operational performance and identifies best practices to drive our company forward and benefit our customers at every level.

Project Delivery

Aligned to Project Management Institute (PMI) best practices, the CoE delivery assurance processes are designed to optimise value and project performance. Our world class Project Assurance Review Process provides a comprehensive and structured independent review at each critical stage gate on our projects and supports our objective of delivering projects to the highest standards for our clients.

Technical Assurance

We maintain detailed technical libraries and a network of experts, both within the organisation and the industry’s leading bodies. Access to this body of knowledge allows us to offer customers leading-edge technical services at every stage of their project.

Continuous Improvement

The CoE drives us towards a culture of continuous improvement within Lean Automation to deliver maximum value across all our businesses. Our Lean Six Sigma Academy provides a framework to train Green Belt Six Sigma specialists and support our lean and agile change programs throughout our organisation.

Knowledge Management

With our wealth of technical expertise and knowhow, we have established technical communities of practice, which helps us build on success, combine capabilities and ensures the Lean Automation name stands above all others.

Quality Assurance

The CoE acts as the home for Quality at Lean Automation, focusing on ISO Certification, a world class Integrated Management System, Project Assurance Review and Lessons Learned. This enables our global teams to benefit from the positive and challenging experiences of others in our organisation.

Innovation & Digital Solutions

Our leading operational performance is underpinned by our laser focus on the identification and deployment of digital solutions within our global operations teams. We are at the forefront in developing in-house digitally engineered applications to enhance our performance.
