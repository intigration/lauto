Ennova AI

Ennova AI, is Lean Automation’s AI-powered platform that redefines industrial asset management across the entire asset lifecycle. Built with unparalleled flexibility and depth, Ennova AI supports both SaaS and on-premises deployments to meet the unique operational needs of the energy sector, seamlessly integrating with every stage—from design and construction to commissioning, operations, and maintenance.

Ennova AI brings data and operations to life with immersive walkthrough simulations, live streaming, and real-time visualisations, making complex decision-making faster and more intuitive. The platform’s dynamic design reviews, advanced analytics, and construction sequencing streamline processes, while its Asset Information Management (AIM), remote monitoring, and autonomous operations empower operators with the precision and confidence needed for high-performance operations. Featuring our industrial co-pilot powered by conversational AI, Ennova AI offers next-level support for operational productivity.

Ennova AI integrates effortlessly with third-party systems and amplifies its capabilities through Lean Automation’s proprietary AI solutions, including the Virtual Flow Meter, Advanced Process Control, AI Autopilot, and a suite of digital twins.
