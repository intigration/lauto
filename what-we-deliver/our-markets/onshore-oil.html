Our Markets

Onshore Oil

Lean Automation is at the heart of helping the sector change from within. We’re partnering with our clients to provide lower carbon solutions for conventional sources in order to secure a smooth transition in the coming decades.

Our expertise

With over 100 years' experience in the onshore industry, we’ve worked with every oil variant, from heavy to light oils, high sulphur fields and high pressure.

Our expertise covers:

Conventional oil production and processing facilities
In-situ oil facilities using Steam Assisted Gravity drainage (SAGD)
Central processing plants
Oil separation
Gathering and field facilities, including well pads
Oil treatment
Steam and production pipelines
Upgrading
Sulphur handling and management
CO₂ and Sour (H₂S) gas/liquids treating
Sulphur removal and recovery
Sweet and sour gas field development
Gas dewpoint
LPG/NGL recovery and fractionation
Cryogenics, deep-cut ethane rejection and recovery
Utilities and offsite
Modularisation
Debottleneck projects
Revamps and upgrades
Expansion projects
Long-term sustaining capital projects

Together, we deliver

Front end advisory (FEL 0-3)

  • Front End Design
  • Feasibility studies and conceptual design
  • Site evaluation and selection studies
  • Technology evaluation & selection (including evaluation of emerging technologies)
  • Assess the commercial and technological risks of projects including project execution risk assessment for brownfield and green field facilities
  • Guide developers through the government approval process including consenting, permitting and advisory services
  • Strategies for optimizing greenfield layouts and brownfield layout integration with existing facilities and site constraints
  • Preparations of CapEx and OpEx cost estimates for FID based on our “as-built” experience

Engineering & delivery phase

  • Detailed engineering including structural, process, mechanical, electrical, control & instrument, safety
  • PMC, owners engineering, EPCM and project management services
  • Construction and commissioning activities
  • Fabrication yard supervision as required

Operations & maintenance phase

  • Advisory services
  • O&M execution
  • Operator training programs
