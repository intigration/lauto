Our Markets

Waste To Chemicals, Energy & Fuels

Lean Automation’s sustainability strategy has been developed to infiltrate every strand of our business globally. Part of our commitment involves putting our expertise to good use to help reduce humanity’s impact to our world’s climate.

Worldwide Experience to Support the Circular Economy

Through our extensive worldwide experience, we advise, engineer and deliver services to support the circular economy, through feedstock processing and conversion (e.g. gasification, pyrolysis), and in the clean-up processes and upgrading to create fuels and chemical products.

We’ve developed major production, process and refining infrastructure in North and South America, the Middle East, Russia and North Africa.

We provide customers with our commercial expertise for CapEx and OpEx estimates, risk analysis and contract management. From feasibility, through to FEED, EPCM including commissioning, operations, maintenance and decommissioning. In essence, we deliver results across the full lifecycle of an asset.

Project life-cycle services

We support customers every step of the way through their project life cycle from providing technology evaluation / selection, through to detailed design and delivery, project and construction management to post operation support and expert witness. Our services include:

Feasibility studies and conceptual design

Front end designs

Developer engineering

Technology selection studies

(including evaluation of emerging technologies)

Provision of CapEx and OpEx estimates

Full engineering, procurement, construction management, and commissioning services

Owner’s engineer and project management team

Operations and maintenance
