Our Markets

Offshore Oil

Economic Challenges

Enhancing recovery from existing and new oil fields, often in difficult environments and uncertain market conditions, whilst doing all we can to minimise carbon intensity, presents the industry with many technical and economic challenges. We have the breadth and depth of expertise, the intellect and the drive to overcome these challenges and help our clients to provide the world with the energy it needs today, in the most responsible way ever.

Topsides

Designing, project managing and maintaining facilities with a view to minimising downtime and improving safety and performance

  • Process and facilities engineering
  • Subsea and flow assurance
  • Independent structural analysis and verification
  • Safety, reliability and human factors
  • Fluid mechanics and CFD
  • Hydrodynamics

Subsea

Combining cross-disciplinary skills and experience to solve challenges within deep water developments

  • Flow assurance
  • Pipeline and subsea engineering
  • Materials and corrosion engineering
  • Integrity management
  • Risk and reliability

Field Development

Making an impact at the earliest opportunity

  • Process and facilities engineering
  • Subsea and flow assurance
  • Independent structural analysis and verification
  • Safety, reliability and human factors
  • Fluid mechanics and CFD
  • Hydrodynamics
  • Many other areas of technical performance

Floating Production Systems

Delivering successful solutions in bespoke form, we’re recognised in the industry for our expertise in naval architecture, marine systems, structural engineering, facilities layout, risers, mechanical engineering, electrical and instrumentation and safety engineering.

The core of our capabilities lies in the detailed execution of:

  • FPSO’s
  • Semisubmersibles
  • TLPs
  • Spars
  • Paired-Column Semisubmersible
  • Other specialised designs such as Flat Plate 40 (FP-40) Spar and DDPSO (Deep Draught Production, Storage and Offloading)

Our Software Tools

We have developed a suite of tried and tested in-house software tools as well as industry standard tools to optimise design and operations.

01

MeasVal

Performs QA check of HYSYS model inputs, outputs and provides error identification

02

MeasCap

Capacity model linked to HYSYS allows multiple facility production cases to be run and constraints to be identified and assessed quickly

03

Prorank

Methodology to compare and rank projects/modifications on an equivalent economic basis

04

Override Assessment Tool

Allows operators to quickly assess the risk of multiple related overrides and understand the implications and related safeguards

05

HAZOP Action Close-out Management

Web-based HAZOP management tool tracks and controls action progress and provides full visibility across multiple organisations

06

Debottlenecking Database

Used to quickly identify common options for debottlenecking and allow a rapid risk assessment on additional impacts
