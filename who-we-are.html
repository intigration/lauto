About Us

Who We Are

We fuse our deep expertise in Artificial Intelligence and industrial automation with a relentless commitment to Lean methodologies. This unique blend allows us to solve complex operational challenges, transforming industries from within. Large enough to offer every skill and capability in AI-driven solutions, yet agile enough to make swift, impactful decisions on the ground—right where they're needed. This empowers our clients to achieve peak performance, ensuring they remain at the forefront of their industries, and solidifies our position as leaders in intelligent industrial optimization.

What We Do

Our Core Expertise: AI-Driven Solutions

We design, build, and maintain the intelligent automation assets that power the world for today and future-proof it for tomorrow. The trust our clients have placed in our ability to deliver AI-driven solutions has fueled our growth. Our ambition is to be the premier partner for industrial AI and Lean Automation. From consulting and design to building, commissioning, and continuous optimization, we provide end-to-end capabilities.

Health & Safety: Our Zero Harm Commitment

The welfare of our people, our planet, and our assets is a fundamental core value at Lean Automation. We are committed to achieving Zero Harm every day, encompassing both physical and psychological well-being. Establishing environments where risk is understood and controlled is critical to this aim, empowering everyone to prioritize safety without hesitation.

At Lean Automation, we believe positive safety outcomes are a result of a strong culture and effective leadership, where everyone shares responsibility for safeguarding our operations.
