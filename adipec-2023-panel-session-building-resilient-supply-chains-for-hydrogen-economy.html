ADIPEC 2023: Panel Session - Building resilient supply chains for hydrogen economy

John Kent - Chief Energy Transition Officer
Wednesday, 4 October 2023 | 11:00 -12:00 GST | Panel Session | ICC Hall

Hear from John Kent, our Chief Energy Transition Officer, about how industry leaders are building resilient supply chains to support the hydrogen economy as he shares his insights on a strategic panel at ADIPEC 2023.

Dive deep into the challenges and opportunities of developing a robust hydrogen supply chain. From pipelines to transport solutions, and large-scale storage facilities, understand the critical role these elements play in integrating hydrogen into our global energy mix.

Don't miss this chance to learn from one of the leading voices in the field and be part of the conversation.
