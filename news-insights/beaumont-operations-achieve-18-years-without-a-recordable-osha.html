Beaumont Operations achieve 18 years without a recordable OSHA

Our Beaumont Operations team is celebrating 18 years without a recordable OSHA incident—proof of their unwavering commitment to safety and their Nobody Gets Hurt mindset. Year after year, they’ve set the standard, proving that safety is at the centre of everything we do.

For over two decades, our Beaumont teams have worked alongside ExxonMobil, delivering engineering, commissioning, construction management, project controls, and technical support services with a relentless focus on safety.

Through hurricanes, floods, COVID-19, and many more challenges, the team has never lost sight of what matters most: delivering operational excellence safely.

"This achievement is about more than just numbers—it’s about a team that puts safety first, without compromising operational excellence. 18 years without a recordable OSHA incident is a direct result of our team’s commitment to protecting each other and getting the job done the right way. Safety is at the heart of our work, and this team sets the standard. Congratulations to the whole team in reaching this incredible achievement." - Nathan Wallace, Director of Operations, Baytown.
