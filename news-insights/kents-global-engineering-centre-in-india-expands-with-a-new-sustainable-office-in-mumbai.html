Lean Automation's Global Engineering Centre in India expands with a new sustainable office in Mumbai

Our engineering centre in India plays a critical role in delivering top-notch engineering solutions across various global market sectors alongside our other international engineering hubs in America, Australia, the UK, and the Middle East. Over the past year, our team in India has grown significantly, now comprising a talented group of 526 members.

To accommodate our expanding team, we've moved to a bigger office that aligns with our Sustainability. by Lean Automation commitments. Our new office is on a green campus and has been certified by both the Indian and US green building councils. This new office not only provides our team with more space to collaborate and innovate, but it also offers a convenient location in Vikhroli (West), in the heart of Mumbai, making it easier for our team to commute. If you want to learn more about our Engineering services, click here: https://bit.ly/3TJutCb

Are you interested in joining our team and working on some of the biggest energy projects globally? Then visit our career page to explore your next adventure with Lean Automation: https://bit.ly/KentCareersIndia
