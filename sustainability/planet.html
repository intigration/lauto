Planet

Sustainability Pillar 2

Supporting a Thriving Planet

We’re committed to being a compassionate catalyst for the energy transition. Addressing the wounds of yesterday, powering the world today and making it future-ready for tomorrow.

Our focus

Accelerating the energy transition

We’re using our agility and know-how to help our clients break new ground in renewable energy, whilst rolling up our sleeves and tackling the tough work of decarbonising conventional energy sources from within.

By 2027, a third of our revenue will come from activities associated with the production of low carbon and renewable energy sources
Develop a KPI and tracking dashboard for all major projects on how they have contributed to decarbonisation
Develop our next generation of digital tools and systems that allow us to monitor and measure the carbon handprint of our projects / designs
Explore how we are accelerating the energy transition
Decarbonisation & Renewables Expertise
Our focus

Tackling climate change from within

We’ve looked within to create bold but credible science-led net-zero plans that focus on rapid, deep and achievable emission cuts for us, our suppliers and our employees.

Net zero across scopes 1, 2 and 3 by 2040
Undertake a gap analysis against the Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures (TCFD) recommendations and develop an action plan to prepare for disclosure
30% reduction in scope 1 and 2 emissions by 2027 from our 2022 baseline
This is how we are tackling climate change from within
Our focus

Reducing our environmental impact

We’re taking daily responsibility to reduce our use of plastic, waste and water, as well as raising awareness of key issues such as protecting and nurturing biodiversity.

Develop a suite of environmental awareness and training resources
Eliminate single use plastics from our operations
Establish a waste disposal baseline and develop a reduction strategy
Establish a water usage baseline and develop a reduction strategy
Discover how we are reducing our environmental impact
