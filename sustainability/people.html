Sustainability Pillar 1

Empowering People to Prosper

We believe the meaningful change we all seek begins from within. We encourage a collective energy built on individual actions to create an inclusive, safe and prosperous world for our people and the communities in which we operate.

Our focus

Nurturing brilliant minds

Were attracting, educating and developing a smart, brave, agile workforce so that the brightest minds can create a better today, and an even brighter tomorrow.

Create a bespoke and supportive career development framework for employees to thrive
Develop and deliver an education programme to support skills migration to deliver the energy transition
Establish a clear competency framework to deliver rich career paths
This is how we are nurturing brilliant minds
Our focus

Lifting communities

Were lifting the communities where we operate by employing local people and local suppliers, educating the next generation of talent, and providing donations and community development.

Build a best practise toolbox for local content
Create a system to track & collate community investment
Develop a local content strategy
Develop a standard around community investment
Develop standard metrics for what we can track on local content
Offer our teams sunshine days to support local causes
Find out how we are lifting communities
Our focus

Empowering our people

Were empowering our employees to prosper by providing them with the financial, physical, mental and emotional knowledge and support they need to thrive.

Develop, pilot and implement weekly mood 'pop' surveys
Increase the utilisation rate of our Employee Assistance Programme
Integrate wellbeing into the inclusion and belonging standards
Discover how we are empowering our people
