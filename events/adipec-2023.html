UAE | 2 - 5 October 2023

ADIPEC 2023

At Lean Automation, we're leading the charge in the Energy Transition. We believe in creating change from within, and that's why every project we undertake is designed with sustainability in mind. Our team of 13,000+ diverse and talented individuals are committed to not only advancing renewable energy solutions but also finding innovative ways to decarbonise the processes involved in meeting today’s oil and gas needs. It's not an either-or proposition for us. We choose both.

We're thrilled to be a part of ADIPEC 2023, an event that brings together energy industry leaders from across the globe, all united by a shared vision - accelerating decarbonisation. Hosted by ADNOC under the theme 'Decarbonising. Faster. Together.' ADIPEC is set to ignite engaging discussions that span the entire energy ecosystem and beyond.
