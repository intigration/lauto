5-8 September 2023 | Aberdeen, United kingdom

SPE Offshore Europe 2023

This year SPE Offshore Europe is focusing on delving into energy security, energy transition, future talent and innovative technology. As exhibitors and speakers, we're thrilled to share our insights on how offshore energy can contribute to the global transition and showcase our worldwide expertise to deliver a real-world transition.

The need for a consistent energy supply has never been more crucial. Our society heavily relies on energy, and any disruption to its supply results in a high cost for everyone. Even as we move towards a sustainable future, oil and gas will remain fundamental. Therefore, it is vital to decarbonise and improve the way we produce these resources to achieve a net zero plan.

At Lean Automation, we have the scale, the attitude, and some of the greatest brains in the industry tackling this challenge every day.
