5-8 September 2022

Gastech, Fiera Milano, Italy

The global energy landscape is changing - join us at Gastech Milan 2022: The heart of the natural gas, LNG, hydrogen, and low-carbon solutions conversation

Renowned as one of the most significant meeting places for the upstream, midstream and downstream gas and LNG professionals Gastech will be celebrating its 50th anniversary in Milan, an international centre of business with a thriving gas industry.

Ideally timed 2-months ahead of COP-27, the four-day exhibition and conference will provide a forum for exhibitors, sponsors and speakers, to showcase to a global audience, their solutions to the challenges faced on the journey to net-zero.

Building on the success of Gastech in Dubai 2021, we are delighted to participate this year once again to not only showcase who we are and our extended capabilities but to connect with current and new clients and partners and explore how we can continue to support the drive towards a cleaner energy future.

Attending Gastech and want to meet our team? Email us at bd@kentplc.com to arrange a meeting or drop by our stand.
